Save the Children

Indonesia has suffered a lot from earthquakes, tsunamis, volcanic eruptions, floods and political instability. The economy is struggling nad the poverty is widespreaded. Malnutrition causes tons of children to have stunted growth, many of them die under the age of five and child labour is still going on there! However, you can make a change and help those children to have educations and have a better future ahead of them!

Caritas

This Catholic Church, helped people around the world and supports countries including Tajikistan. They even cleaned water for around 9000 people in Jomi, Tajikistan and provide medical help to children's hospitals. Children with various illnesses, such as cancer, receiver their help and the Church aims to spread love, through their small projects.

Care and Save the Children

There are 27% of children in Mozambique that are not able to receive any education, 22% are caught into child labour, 55% of girls have trouble in reading and writing and 46% of people live in poverty! At the moment, Save the Children takes care of emergency situations in Mozambique, as it has been by multiple powerful cyclones lately, resulting them being in an emergency situation! Care helps women in Mozambique and protects them from being abused in various ways and supports refugees, children and people affected by natural disasters. You could even help to protect Nigerian children, as many school kids have reportedly been sold for as little as $12!
