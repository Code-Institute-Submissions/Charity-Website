Lowest GDP Per Capita In The World (Projected).

The chart shows the countries with the lowest GDP per capita in the world. This indicates that the Demcratic Republic of Congo has the lowest standards of living, as a low gross domestic product means that there is not enough output in the economy for the country's entire population.

The chart above shows the values of the lowest gini coefficient values in the world. This indicates how unequally the countries' income is distributed per 20% of the labour forces, which causes poor people to remain poor and wealthy people to stay wealthy.
